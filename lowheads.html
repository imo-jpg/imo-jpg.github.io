Work: Low Heads

In the fall of 2020, I developed a new logo for the undeground streetwear curator Low Heads. Building off an existing web presence and style, I worked with their founder to come up with a mark that felt unique to the brand yet took a back seat to the creators and makers that Low Heads promotes.
